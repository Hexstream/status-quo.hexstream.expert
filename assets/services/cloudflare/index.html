⚓ Usage notes

As of 1 august 2022, I have migrated ~100% of my web infrastructure to Cloudflare Pages, Workers, R2 and Registrar. Highly recommended!
(These products are coming out of beta later in 2022.)

I use Wrangler for development and deployment, and rclone to manage R2.
